Services

We specialize in comprehensive Custom Engine Design and Development. Our engineering team creates bespoke engines meticulously tailored to meet your specific performance goals and precise weight requirements. This focused approach ensures we deliver powerful engines optimized not only for dominant straight-line stability but also for exceptional agility and high-speed cornering ability.

Tuning

Unlock your engine's full potential with our professional ECU Tuning service. Using our dedicated Dyno Bench, we perform expert calibration on leading Racing ECUs, including aRacer, UMA, and Pitsbike. This specialized tuning ensures your engine delivers the highest level of performance tailored precisely to your needs.

About Us

Originally established as DNS MAIN ("Dale Na Sir"), our brand has strategically evolved to Neck Shot Racing. This change was implemented to enhance our market appeal and strengthen our connection with customers and fans. Neck Shot Racing embodies our competitive spirit and winning attitude. Our signature gesture pointing a finger at the neck is performed when we successfully outpace and leave competitors behind. It serves as a confident symbol of speed and supremacy on the track.
